Hi, I’m Opu. Nice to meet you.

I am a versatile and driven professional with a strong foundation in mechanical engineering, robotics, and international trade. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from Daffodil International University, along with a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Chongqing University of Science and Technology, specializing in design, manufacturing, and automation. My academic journey emphasized practical innovation, where I worked on projects such as reverse engineering automotive components and developing a collaborative surgical robot, strengthening my expertise in CAD/CAM, 3D modeling, and industrial design.

I have over four years of diverse professional experience. I began my career as a Mechanical Design Engineer, focusing on product design, simulation, and technical problem-solving, and later worked as a Technical Support Engineer at GeoVision Inc., where I supported global clients, delivered training, and contributed to international operations. Currently, I serve as a Foreign Trade Specialist at Hifi Lite International, managing sourcing, supplier negotiations, and global sales. I am also the Founder of Ditizen Co. Ltd., where I provide complete sourcing solutions from China, along with academic support services through Ditizen EduSolve.

Alongside my professional career, I am pursuing a master’s degree focused on collaborative robotics in surgery, combining engineering with medical technology. I am actively involved in robotics research and development, with hands-on experience in inspection robots and hardware systems. Passionate about continuous learning and innovation, I aim to contribute to advancements in robotics and automation while building impactful global business solutions.
